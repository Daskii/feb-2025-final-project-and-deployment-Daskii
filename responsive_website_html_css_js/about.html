About Garments by Daskii

Welcome to Garments by Daskii, your premier South African fashion destination. We are dedicated to offering you a curated selection of stylish, high-quality clothing and accessories that celebrate the vibrant culture and elegance of South Africa.

Founded in 2025, Garments by Daskii was born out of a passion for fashion and a commitment to providing our customers with the best shopping experience. From everyday essentials to luxurious pieces, we bring you a diverse range of products that cater to every style and occasion.

Our Brands

  • Spirit: A contemporary brand that embodies the energy and creativity of South African fashion. Spirit offers trendy, affordable clothing for the modern individual.
  • Tasiki: Our luxurious brand, Tasiki, represents sophistication and elegance. Each piece is crafted with premium materials and meticulous attention to detail, perfect for those who appreciate the finer things in life.
